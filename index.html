Welcome to my website (currently under construction)!

I am an Associate Professor in the Economics Department at the University of Oxford and a Fellow of University College.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Organizational Economics, Monetary Policy, and Empirical Methods. I approach questions in these areas using both theory models and novel empirical tools, including those from machine learning and data science.
